Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words



There are many considerations to consider when it comes to cyber security in a company. It will depend on whether the company's employees mostly work with PCs and how many employees the company has.

If we start from the fact that the company mainly works with PCs and that there is a fair number of employees, the first security measure would be to run security training/courses in the form of simple introductory videos with questions for all employees that will be mandatory, so that the risk of phishing and other specific security threats is reduced. Make all employees in the company care about cyber security, not just the IT department.

It will be very important to have good security systems and programs that limit the risk of the company being exposed to Cross-Site scripting, SQL injection, and Cross-Site request forgery. Good training and information in this area is very important.

Good login with two-step login and routines for changing passwords is also important to avoid unnecessary threats to the system and employees. Strict rules for which programs can be installed on a work PC will reduce the risk of installing malware on employees' work PCs. Random samples of e-mails that simulate phishing attempts will make the employees more secure and aware in their everyday work.

Those are only a small number of measures that can help a business with cybersecurity.
